Hi! My name is Patrick Dugan and I'm currently a designer at Meta working on Messenger. I'm also building Hikearound, the easiest way to discover, save, and share great local hikes.
I previously worked on design teams at the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, Quora, Nextdoor and Google as an individual contributor and people manager. I was also a member of the Designer Fund's professional development program, Bridge.
I live in Seattle and attended the University of Washington where I studied Human Computer Interaction.
I enjoy running, camping, writing, listening to records, and working on my Harley.
