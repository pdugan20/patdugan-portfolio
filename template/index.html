Hi! My name is Patrick Dugan and I'm currently a member of the design team at the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative.
I previously was a member of and led design teams at Quora, Nextdoor and Google and was a member of the Designer Fund's professional development program, Bridge.
I live in San Francisco and attended the University of Washington in Seattle where I studied Human Computer Interaction.
